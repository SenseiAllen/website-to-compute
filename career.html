Lee Sang-hyeok (Faker) is a South Korean League of Legends player for the team T1, formerly known as SKT Telecom T1. He plays as the teams resident mid-laner and has been since 2013. Faker started his pro career in 2013 and garnered the attention of many viewers and pro players due to his agressive play style and high skill mechanics. Throughout the years he has led the team in multiple victories domestically and internationally. Most notable being the only five-time League of Legends World Champion. Due to his numerous accomplishments, he is considered to be the best League of Legends player in the world, with no one coming close to his crown. In present day, he is still considered to be the greatest of all time and his impact on the E-sports scene will be forever immortlized in gaming history.
